Exchange Rate Comparison

Compare Mastercard rates with live market rates and quickly spot better exchange opportunities. Mastercard exchange rates are updated only a few times a day. When the market moves significantly, this can create opportunities to make purchases or cash advances at a more favorable rate than the live market rate.

This dashboard automatically retrieves Mastercard rates via API and compares them with near real-time exchange rates from Yahoo Finance. On Mastercard’s official site, these rates can only be checked manually each time, but this page makes the comparison visible instantly.
